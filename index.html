Campaign

This is a campaign message promoting collaboration, unity, and cooperation using the term "USAnergy" and vibrant colors to represent the diversity of the student body.


MESSAGE OF THE CAMPAIGN

Synergy in Diversity

The key message of the campaign is "Synergy in Diversity." The campaign team believes that despite our differences, we can still work together to achieve a common goal. The campaign will emphasize the importance of collaboration, unity, and cooperation, and highlight how these values can benefit the student body as a whole. The campaign will use the term "USAnergy" to convey this message of unity and collaboration.
